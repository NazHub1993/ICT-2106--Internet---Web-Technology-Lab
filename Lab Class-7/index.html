Nestled between hills and sea, Chittagong thrives as Bangladesh’s major port city. The bustling Karnaphuli River flows through its heart, powering trade and life. From Patenga Beach to Foy's Lake, nature and culture harmoniously coexist.

Comilla: Rich in heritage, Comilla is home to ancient sites like Mainamati’s Buddhist ruins. The calm of Comilla’s countryside blends with its growing urban charm. Its history, cuisine, and academic legacy make it a cultural gem of Bangladesh.

Set beside the serene Brahmaputra River, Mymensingh boasts natural beauty and calm. Known for its rich literary and educational heritage, it houses the historic Ananda Mohan College and BAU. A blend of tradition and modernity, Mymensingh reflects the soul of rural Bengal.
